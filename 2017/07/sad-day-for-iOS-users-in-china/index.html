Sad Day for iOS Users in China

Posted on 2017-07-29 Edited on 2023-09-27 In Life Disqus:

Today is a sad day for iOS users in China who have set their iTunes store mainly in China. Because all the iOS apps including Surge, Shadowrocket and Potatso which can go through the great firewall have been pulled out of the iTunes store of China.

@Blankwonder, the creator of Surge:

Surge has been pulled out of iTunes store of China. We are considering solutions, please wait for the further information.

Surge 在中国区被 Apple 主动下架。相关应对方案正在考虑中，请等待进一步消息。

— Yachen Liu (@Blankwonder) 2017年7月29日

@icodesign_me, the creator of Potatso:

Good Bye.

再见！ pic.twitter.com/IRwxpeSFRH

— icodesign (@icodesign_me) 2017年7月29日

30 years ago, the first email of China was sent from Beijing to Germany:

Across the Great Wall we can reach every corner in the world.

But 30 years later, we’ve been trapped within the Great Wall and forbidden to connect with the world.

Today is a sad day.
